Sixth Sense Esport

is a Call of Duty: Mobile clan dedicated to having fun. We were founded on Oct 20 2020 as a joint venture between like minded gamers. SSE fosters an environment where clan members and the general public can enjoy their gaming with polite, friendly people, in either a casual or competitive player.

Mision Vision

We, in SIXTH SENSE clan, continuously strive to be the Call of Duty: Mobile gamers who are globally competitive and unconditionally love and respect the gaming community.

Values

Teamwork and respect are the values which Sixth Sense are put into action every single day.

Team

SSE firmly believes that participation, sharing and teamwork are what lets people grow, a team is greather than the sum of its parts. Sixth Sense favours and promotes professional and staff diversity and innovation to ensure maximum results from its team.

Respect

SSE is committed to earning the respect of its members through behaviour inspired by a sense of responsibility and honesty. We respect all legal principles as well as internal and external rules in our work.
